# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in NeuroScope welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying accountable for impact.
- Give feedback that helps the project improve.
- Respect different backgrounds, experience levels, and research contexts.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sharing sensitive biomedical or patient data in project issues, discussions, or pull requests.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants whose behavior harms the community.

## Reporting

If you see or experience unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers through the repository owner profile or a private channel they list for the project.

This code of conduct is adapted from common open-source community standards and tailored for a small research tooling project.
